About Us

Founder & CEO: Ako enow Chérif (Agbor Tabi), Engineer

SIMPLE CAT STUDIO is a Charity Social Cultural Economy and Community development Enterprise with high drive for success.

We are a professional creative hub dedicated to the discovery and promotion of talents.

Our organization provides expert guidance, industry training, and strategic cultural management.

Areas of Operation

Our commitment involves comprehensive community engagement across these sectors:

  • Produce: Creating high-quality cultural content.
  • Promote: Elevating artists and community initiatives.
  • Transform: Driving professional skill development.
  • Commercialize: Connecting talents with market opportunities.
  • Export & Import: Facilitating international cultural exchange.
  • Organize: Managing events and volunteer projects.

Competition

We host regular challenges, talent showcases, and industry networking events.

Stay updated on our latest creative opportunities and application deadlines.

Our Commitment

We aim to reveal hidden talents, develop professional skills, and open doors to new career paths.

Our programs are designed to meet international standards while preserving local cultural richness.
